About Us

Discover our story

A group of college students who were fed up with a lack of affordable dining alternatives on campus created Wontuna. They identified a need in the market and chose to address it by founding a business that provides nutritious food in a convenient manner. The group is committed to ensuring that Wontuna continues to serve the needs of the university community while also being a sustainable business and socially responsible company.

Tuna Wonton

Tuna wontons are a classic snack that everyone can enjoy at any time of the day. It is a snack that is different from others as it offers a unique combination of flavors and textures. The crispiness of the wonton wrapper complements the savory and tender tuna filling inside. These tasty treats can be enjoyed as a snack on their own or as an appetizer to a larger meal. .

Why Choose us?

The quality and quantity of the ingredients used in making our tuna wontons carefully measured and controlled to ensure consistency in every batch. Additionally, the vegetable ingredients are sourced from local farms, ensuring they are free from any harmful chemicals or pesticides and have enough nutrients to complement a balanced diet..
